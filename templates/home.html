Facebook Image Processing Analysis

Hi, our names are Tiffany Hsu, Kevin Jiang, and Jerry Hsu, and we are Team #SELFIE. We are three Yale Sophomores enrolled in the CPSC 437 Databases course. The goal of our project is to analyze the psychology of color as a medium of marketing and branding using data from users and photos on Facebook. In doing so, we will process images for a myriad of content including, but not limited to, dominant RGB color values, saturation, brightness, and contrast. We greatly appreciate your help in providing us access to your Facebook Profile Picture Album for our final project.
